Privacy Policy

REST Client — API Tester  ·  Effective Date: June 14, 2026

👋 Overview

Short version: REST Client does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All API requests you make go directly from your device to the servers you choose. We never see them.

This Privacy Policy describes how Hypertun ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the REST Client — API Tester Android application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📡 Information We Do NOT Collect

The App is designed to function without collecting personal information. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • The API endpoints, request bodies, headers, or responses you send/receive
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data stored in your local request history or templates

All data you enter into the App (URLs, headers, bodies, environments, templates, request history) is stored locally on your device only using an encrypted Room database and is never transmitted to us.

💳 Payment Processing (Stripe)

The App offers optional Pro subscriptions processed by Stripe, Inc. If you choose to subscribe, your payment information (card number, billing address) is entered directly into Stripe's secure interface and is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We receive from Stripe only:

  • A confirmation of payment success/failure
  • A Stripe Customer ID (anonymous token)

We do not receive or store your card number, CVV, or full billing address.

🔄 Restore Purchase (Firebase)

The App uses Google Firebase (Firestore & Auth) solely for the "Restore Purchase" feature. When you use this feature, your email address is used to look up your existing Pro subscription across devices.

  • Your email is only used to verify your purchase — it is not used for marketing
  • Firebase data is governed by Google Firebase's Privacy Policy
  • If you never use "Restore Purchase", Firebase is not contacted

🌐 Internet Permission

The App requires the INTERNET permission exclusively to execute the HTTP requests you initiate. The App sends your API requests directly from your device to the destination server you specify. We do not proxy, log, or inspect these requests in any way.

🔒 Data Security

  • Request history, environments, and templates are stored in a local Room (SQLite) database on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server owned or operated by us
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data

👶 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

🔗 Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services. Please review their respective policies:

  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Google Firebase — Purchase restore (Auth & Firestore)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
